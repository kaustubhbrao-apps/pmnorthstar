The LEGO turnaround is a masterclass in **Operational Focus.** CEO Jørgen Vig Knudstorp famously said that LEGO had 'lost its way' by trying to be everything to everyone. He saved the company by reminding them that their value wasn't in the theme parks or the clothes—it was in the **System of the Brick.**
