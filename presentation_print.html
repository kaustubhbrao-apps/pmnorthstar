Product Strategy: Value Prop Canvas

Applying the Value Proposition Canvas to ensure perfect product-market fit. By deeply mapping the specific frustrations of aspiring Product Managers against our core features, we create a highly differentiated pedagogical moat that legacy ed-tech cannot replicate.

Customer Profile

Customer Pains:

  • Passive Learning: Most resources rely on static reading, failing to build execution skills.
  • Paywalled: Entry-level Indian cohorts charge ₹15k-₹40k just for foundational theory.

Customer Gains:

  • Pass PM job interviews with absolute confidence.
  • Learn by doing through actual product decisions.

Our Value Map

Pain Relievers:

  • Active Execution: We force users to make strategic decisions via branching simulations.
  • 100% Free: Removing the upfront financial barrier for entry-level talent.

Gain Creators:

  • Immediate, operator-level feedback on every decision.
  • Direct recruitment pipelines to enterprise tech companies.

Operations: The SIPOC Model

To sustain a high volume of output (10+ assets per week) with a lean team, we structure our operational pipeline strictly using the SIPOC Model (Suppliers, Inputs, Process, Outputs, Customers). This standardized workflow ensures quality control and eliminates bottlenecks prior to scaling.

S
Suppliers

Public tech companies, startup blogs, and competitor platforms.

I
Inputs

Public earnings data, UX screenshots, and product launch notes.

P
Process

Synthesis into bulleted markdown and automated publishing.

O
Outputs

Deep-dive case studies and interactive branching simulations.

C
Customers

Aspiring Product Managers and B2B Enterprise HR departments.
