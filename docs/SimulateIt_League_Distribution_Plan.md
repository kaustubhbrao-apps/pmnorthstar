# 🎬 Northstar Simulation League: Master Distribution Playbook

> **The Vision:** This isn't a generic feature announcement. This is a cinematic, high-stakes tournament for product builders. The aesthetic is *Kumarmood*: moody, fast, sleek, and slightly intimidating. We are selling the **tension** of being a founder.

---

## 🎥 The Production Bible (The "Kumarmood" Aesthetic)
* **Lighting/Visuals:** Dark mode interface screens. Deep blacks with the signature Northstar red (`#D4102F`) and the new neon shimmer gradient piercing through the darkness. Heavy use of zooming, panning, and micro-animations on the UI.
* **Audio:** Fast, heavy bass, dark synth-wave or aggressive phonk instrumentals. The beat drops *exactly* when the decision node branches or the score flashes. No generic corporate lo-fi.
* **Typography:** Bold, massive JetBrains Mono or Space Grotesk overlapping the video. Fast, kinetic pop-ins. Never more than 3 words on screen at a time.
* **Pacing:** 0-3 seconds: Hook (The Crisis). 3-6 seconds: The Stakes (The UI). 6-10 seconds: The Call to Action (The Drop).

## 🛠️ Execution Checklist for the Video Editor
1. **Never show the exact right answer.** Always cut the video *right* as the user is hovering over the hardest choice.
2. **Use the Figma assets.** The newly pushed `FeaturedDrillCard` with the shimmer border is your money shot. Put it at the end of every video.
3. **Sound design is 60% of the video.** Every UI click, branch expansion, and point tally should have a satisfying, mechanical sound effect (think heavy mechanical keyboard switches).
4. **Keep it under 12 seconds.** Retention is everything. Make the viewer watch it twice to read the text.
